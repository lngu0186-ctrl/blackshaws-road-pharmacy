# Stock Image Shortlist — Blackshaws Road Pharmacy

Prepared during the final polish pass so the site can be upgraded from generic imagery to licensed pharmacy/community-health photography later.

## Selection criteria

- Real community pharmacy feel rather than hospital imagery
- Diverse local-family / everyday-health scenarios
- Clean, premium, well-lit retail look
- Useful across hero banners, service cards, and trust sections
- Commercial licensing available from major stock providers

## Recommended directions

### 1) Pharmacist helping a patient at the counter
Best use: homepage hero, services hero, trust sections

## Suggested placements

- **Homepage hero:** pharmacist + patient / consultation counter image
- **Services page:** consultation or vaccination image crop
- **Shop page:** premium retail shelving / wellness aisle image
- **About / trust areas:** candid patient-support photography, preferably older adult + pharmacist

## Notes

- No direct stock downloads were committed during this pass.
- Existing site visuals remain deployable without these assets.
- When licensing, prefer one cohesive shoot/source to keep lighting, styling and ethnicity mix consistent across the site.
